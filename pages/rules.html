Rules

Basic Rules for game

  1. 9x9: The game is played on a 9x9 grid, which is divided into nine 3x3 smaller grids called "regions"
  2. Fulfill: Your need to fill all empty cells with numbers from 1 to 9. There already some numbers exist.
  3. Fulfill Rule: The most important:
    • Each row must contain the numbers 1 to 9 without repetition
    • Each column must contain the numbers 1 to 9 without repetition
    • Each 3x3 region must contain the numbers 1 to 9 without repetition
  4. logic games:Use your logic to fulfill Sudoku
